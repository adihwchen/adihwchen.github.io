how

away

因為一陣確診案例高峰，台灣在 2021 年 5 月 14 日宣布雙北第三級警戒，不到一週再擴大至全國第三級警戒。宅在家多出許多時間，決定進行一直很想作但總是沒時間、缺動力的事：學畫畫。

水管是朋友充滿性格的寵物狗狗，之前聽了一些軼事覺得太有趣，那天看到照片突然覺得是很適合練習畫畫的題材。直覺是對的，在攝影師隨行掌鏡紀錄之下，水管各種表情跟狀態都非常到位，完全是天生模特。

一天一張畫於焉展開。

Due to a rare spike in domestic pandemic cases, The CECC announced the nationwide Level 3 alert for COVID-19 since May 14, 2021. I decided to start one thing that I've longed to do but have no time or limited motivation in the past: Learn to draw.

Waterpipe is the lovely pet dog of a friend. He has abundant charateristics that made a lot of stories. Started with a shared picture of Waterpipe which I thought it would be great subject to practice. And he does is the perfect model with various looks, thanks to the photographer.

And so it begins, the journey of one Waterpipe a day.
